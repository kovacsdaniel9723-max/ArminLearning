08 Voice Roadmap Prompt

ROLE: Technical product architect

GOAL:
Define a clear voice feature roadmap for a children's learning app.

VERSION 1 - Fake Voice Interaction
- Sound detection only
- Confidence building
- No speech recognition

VERSION 2 - Word-Level Recognition
- Android SpeechRecognizer
- Recognize full words only
- Hungarian language
- Soft failure handling

VERSION 3 - Advanced Pronunciation
- Phoneme-level recognition
- Custom ML model
- Offline capable
- Not part of MVP

OUTPUT:
- Technical decision notes
- Risk assessment
- When to move to next version
